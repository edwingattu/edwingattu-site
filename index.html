The arc

My career doesn’t trace a line — it describes a circle. I started with a B.Tech in Computer Science, then spent a decade working in rooms: recording studios, broadcast suites, game engines, and building sites. Each room taught me the same thing differently, that a feeling is evoked through almost every human interaction. From that point on, Sound design became spatial. Interior design became temporal. Branding became emotional architecture. The obsession with how things feel inside an experience — whether it’s a living room, a game level, or a radio ad — never changed. 23ft. and 23Hz are where that obsession is currently housed.
